Imagine a complex projects with complex architectures. Imagine having to rebuild one of those projects's features because it has multiples integrations that your project doesn't need or because you see a potential security flaws being developed with this new integration.
<br>
Imagine having to deal with the complexity of another integrations because there's no Api Governance and standards to be followed.

<br>
<br>

Api Gateways have a very important role in projects specially when it comes to big companies. Big companies have lots of internal automation and partnership with another
companies. It's imporant to keep track of what's available to use between the teams inside and outside the company and build architectures for security and performance monitoring. 
<br>
<br>

Gateways can be present at any of the seven layers of the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OSI_model" target="blank">OSI Model</a>.
<br>
Api Gateways operates on the Application Layer (Layer 7) where the Http/Https communication happens.
